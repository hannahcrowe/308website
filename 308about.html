About The Whisked to the Peaks Experience

Welcome to Whisked to the Peaks, where culinary excellence reaches new heights and every dining experience is an ascent into carefully curated flavors and breathtaking views. Our restaurant is perched atop the majestic peaks of the Shenadoah Mountains.

At Whisked to the Peaks, we redefine elevated dining, both literally and figuratively. Our commitment to culinary excellence extends to a dynamic and ever-evolving menu. We take pride in crafting a dining experience that changes with the seasons, ensuring our guests embark on a fresh and once in a life time journey with every visit.

Our food menu dances with the rhythms of nature, featuring locally sourced, seasonal ingredients that showcase the flavors of the Blue Ridge Mountains. Complementing this, our cocktail bar boasts an ever-changing drink menu, artfully blending seasonal ingredients with innovative mixology techniques.

This dedication to the ebb and flow of nature guarantees that each encounter at Whisked to the Peaks is a unique and unforgettable exploration of taste and atmopshere.
